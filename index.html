Mindset

How I Think About Security

Detection is table stakes. Understanding why an attack worked, what the attacker was after, and how they'll adapt is the actual job.

One phishing kit appearing across five campaigns tells a better story than five closed cases. I focus on patterns across alerts, not individual alert counts.

Repetitive enrichment and correlation should be automated. Analysts should spend their time on decisions, not data cleaning.

Most phishing incidents aren't one-offs. They're evidence of infrastructure worth tracking and campaigns worth attributing.
